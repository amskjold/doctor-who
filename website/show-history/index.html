Show History

Doctor Who is a British television science-fiction series, produced and screened by the British Broadcasting Corporation on theirBBC 1 channel from 1963 to 1989 in its original form, with a new series launched in early 2005. In between the two, there was a one-off television movie co-produced with Universal Pictures and 20th Century Fox Television, screened on the Fox Network in the United States in 1996.
